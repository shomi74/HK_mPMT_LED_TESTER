# Wiring Architecture

## Breadboard Prototype
During early debugging, one resistor per LED may be used to simplify channel verification.

## Final Cap Wiring
The final cap uses:
- one distributed COMMON_ANODE wire
- 19 individual cathode wires

## Final Signal Count
Total connector signals: 20
- 19 LED cathodes
- 1 COMMON_ANODE

## Recommended Harness
Use a 20-conductor ribbon cable or a labeled 20-wire harness between the carrier board and cap.
