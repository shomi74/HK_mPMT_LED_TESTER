# Connector Selection

## Recommended Connector
A 2x10 IDC-style connector is recommended because the system requires exactly 20 signals:
- 19 LED cathode channels
- 1 COMMON_ANODE line

## Example Board Connector
Samtec SBH11-PBPC-D10-RA-BK or equivalent 2x10 right-angle header.

## Design Notes
- Clearly mark pin 1.
- Clearly mark CH0-CH18 and COMMON_ANODE.
- Verify physical pin numbering in the PCB footprint before fabrication.
